Microsoft Teams: USCG Yeoman

Welcome to the Team!

Hello Coast Guard Yeoman and welcome to Microsoft Teams USCG Yeoman. Microsoft Teams offers many functionalities such as online meetings, screen sharing, file sharing and instant messaging. Within the team, there are 6 channels (Administration, Pay and Personnel, Reserves, Travel and Transportation, EPME and SWE Study). Team members can post information, upload documents, create folders and much more. This is a great platform to colaborate and ensure accurate and updated information is being shared.

One of the main channels of the team is SWE Study. Within this channel is stored RPQ / EPQ files, references and the 2020 November SWE Questions Excel spreadsheet. The purpose of this channel is for Yeoman to network about SWE information. In the beginning of January 2021, an online study group will be established.

**Don't forget to download the MS Teams mobile app to receive information on the go**

Setup an Account

Step One: Go to the CVR Self-Service Portal to enable your account, receive your CVR email and reset your password. Use Google Chrome internet browser and your authentication certificate.

Step Two: Within a few minutes, you will receive an automated email. Follow the instructions to setup your account. During the setup, you will enable a Multi Factor Authentication. What this means is after you login with your password you will go through a second authentication either through the Microsoft Authenticator Application or receive a call on your cell phone. It is HIGHLY recommended that you use the cell phone method.

Step Three: Once your account is enabled, send an email or message to YN1 Christopher E. White to be added to the USCG Yeoman Team.
